ABOUT

One of the problems a farmer like my dad face is that it is quite messy and time-consuming to find a right organic fertilizer recipe that match his need. For example, in order to find a right one, he has to go through a number of farming magazines, looking up on Google with different keywords, visiting tons of websites and reaching out to people. Of course, these are the common aspects of searching. However, I firmly believe that technology is the thing human invented to save our time and to make everything more convenient! Thus, I want to develop a website and smartphone application that provide crowd-sourced reviews about DIY fertilizer recipe.

HOW TO USE THIS WEBSITE

Share


Our Share provides form for we all, farmers, to share the fertilizer recipes that we think they are worth sharing to the community! The form includes space for you to share the unique aspects of your recipe from organic/mixed to weather to the problems your recipe solve!

Search


Our Search provides filter for you to find the recipes that best match your needs. The filter includes the problems the you want to solve at your farm, the type of weather, specific plant type and many more!
